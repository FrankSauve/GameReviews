### The Bad üëé
- So much open world slop. Thankfully my coop partner did most of the required slop on his own so that we could progress the story when we we're playing together.
- The character and facial art and animations were pretty rough looking.
- The story is extremely forgettable and generic. I honestly lost all interest in the main story halfway through. 
- Around half the enemies are extremely annoying to fight. For example enemies that dodge everything you do until you do X type of attack. Just not that fun and interactive.
- Enemies become bullet sponges by the end of the game. They would require like 15-20 hits. We just lowered the difficulty at the end because it was just getting tedious.
